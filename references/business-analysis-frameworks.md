# Business Analysis Frameworks Reference

Curated frameworks that inform StackScan's analysis methodology. Referenced by step 1 (create_plan) for structuring the analysis.

## Process Analysis
- **SIPOC** (Supplier, Input, Process, Output, Customer) — map process boundaries
- **Value Stream Mapping** — identify value-add vs waste steps
- **RACI Matrix** — clarify who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed

## Efficiency
- **Lean principles** — eliminate 7 wastes (transport, inventory, motion, waiting, overproduction, overprocessing, defects)
- **Theory of Constraints** — find and address the single biggest bottleneck first
- **Pareto principle** — 80% of waste comes from 20% of causes

## Financial
- **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)** — include hidden costs (training, migration, downtime)
- **Payback period** — time to recoup investment
- **NPV / IRR** — for larger investments, discount future savings

## Change Management
- **ADKAR** — Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement
- **Kotter's 8 steps** — create urgency, build coalition, form vision, communicate, empower, quick wins, consolidate, anchor
